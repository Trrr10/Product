Questions we get asked

Questions we get asked.

Is this safe for daily use?+

Yes. Every ingredient is at or below its safe daily intake level. Natural caffeine at 80mg is equivalent to one espresso. All vitamins are water-soluble and safe at these doses. Ashwagandha KSM-66® is studied for daily use over 8 weeks and beyond.

Can students drink this?+

Yes. The warning applies to children under 16 as standard caffeine advisory. University students and 16+ students are the core audience. The caffeine + L-Theanine combination is specifically what cognitive performance researchers recommend for sustained study sessions.

Is it suitable for vegetarians and vegans?+

Yes. All ingredients are plant-derived or synthesised without animal products. Fully vegetarian and vegan.

Does it contain taurine?+

No. Taurine is absent by design. High-dose taurine in standard energy drinks is associated with cardiac stress in sensitive individuals. We chose better ingredients instead.

Why is there only 12 calories?+

Because there is zero added sugar and zero fat. The 12 calories come from 3g of carbohydrates used as the carrier base for the active ingredients. This is the lowest calorie count of any functional energy drink in this category.
