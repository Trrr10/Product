Questions we get asked

Questions we get asked.

Is this safe for daily use? +

Yes. Every ingredient is at or below its safe daily intake level. Natural caffeine at 80mg is equivalent to one espresso. All vitamins are water-soluble and safe at these doses.

Can students drink this? +

Yes. The caffeine + L-Theanine combination is designed for sustained focus without anxiety, making it suitable for long study sessions.

Is it suitable for vegetarians and vegans? +

Yes. All ingredients are plant-derived or synthesised without animal products. Fully vegetarian and vegan.

Does it contain taurine? +

No. Taurine is absent by design. We focus on ingredients that support sustained performance without unnecessary stimulation.

Why is there only 12 calories? +

Because there is zero added sugar and zero fat. The 12 calories come only from the carrier base used for active ingredients.
